Our Residential Design Process

A step-by-step look at how we collaborate with you to create your ideal home.

  1. 1

    Initial Consultation & Briefing

    Understanding your lifestyle, aspirations, site conditions, and budget requirements in detail.

  2. 2

    Site Analysis & Feasibility

    Evaluating the property's potential and constraints, including zoning, orientation, and topography.

  3. 3

    Schematic Design

    Developing initial design concepts, floor plans, and exterior sketches for your review and feedback.

  4. 4

    Design Development & Material Selection

    Refining the design, detailing key features, selecting finishes, and creating 3D models.

  5. 5

    Permitting & Construction Documents

    Preparing detailed drawings and specifications for contractor bidding and obtaining building permits.

  6. 6

    Construction Support

    Providing oversight during construction to ensure the design intent is realized accurately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our residential design services.

How long does the residential design process typically take?

The timeline varies greatly depending on the project's scope, complexity, and client decision-making speed. Generally, the design phase (up to construction documents) can take anywhere from 3 to 9 months. We can provide a more specific estimate after our initial consultation.

What is the typical cost for residential architectural services?

Our fees are typically structured based on a percentage of the construction cost, a fixed fee, or an hourly rate, depending on the project specifics. We'll discuss fee structures transparently during the initial consultation to find the best fit for your project.

Can you work with my existing contractor?

Absolutely. We are happy to collaborate with your chosen contractor. We can also provide recommendations for reputable builders we have worked with successfully in the past if you need assistance finding one.

Do you handle interior design as well?

While our primary focus is architecture, we often collaborate closely with interior designers or can offer integrated interior architecture services, including space planning, material selection, and custom cabinetry design, depending on the project scope.
