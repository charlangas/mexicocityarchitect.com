Renovating in Mexico City: Architects & Permits

Navigating SEDUVI, finding a DRO, and why you need a local architect even if you have a US license.

Explore roma-norte

Renovating a Porfirian mansion or a mid-century apartment is the dream. But the bureaucracy is real. The Secretaría de Desarrollo Urbano y Vivienda (SEDUVI) oversees all permits.

The Role of the DRO

You need a 'Director Responsable de Obra' (DRO)—a certified engineer/architect liable for the safety of the project. Your US architect cannot sign off on plans here.

Finding Talent

While sites like Upwork exist, high-end renovation requires a studio with local presence and connections to vetted contractors. Always ask for the architect's 'Cédula Profesional' (license).
