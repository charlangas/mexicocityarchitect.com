Navigating Heritage Zoning in Roma Norte

Why INBA protection isn't a death sentence for your renovation project. A guide to adaptive reuse in Catalogued properties.

Explore roma-norte

Roma Norte is defined by its architectural heritage. But for investors, the 'Catalogued Property' designation can feel like a red flag. It shouldn't.

Understanding INBA vs. SEDUVI

The first step is distinguishing between artistic value (INBA) and urban value (SEDUVI). INBA cares about the facade and historical fabric; SEDUVI cares about usage and density.

The Strategy: Invisible Intervention

Our approach at Talacha is to restore the facade to museum quality while modernizing the core. We use steel framing to reinforce the structure internally, allowing for open-plan layouts without touching the historic masonry.
