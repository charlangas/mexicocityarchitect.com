Foreigner's Guide to Buying Property in Mexico City (2026)

Direct ownership is possible. A comprehensive guide to the 'Restricted Zone', SRE permits, and why CDMX is easier than the coast.

Explore polanco

Mexico City has become a prime destination for American citizens seeking investment and lifestyle opportunities. A common misconception is that foreigners cannot own land in Mexico. This is false. The 'Restricted Zone' applies only to borders and coastlines.

Direct Ownership vs. Fideicomiso

Because Mexico City is inland, American citizens can hold Direct Deed (Escritura) to property, exactly like a Mexican citizen. You do NOT need a bank trust (Fideicomiso) here, which saves significantly on annual fees.
