Seismic Safety: What Every Investor Needs to Know

The 2026 Building Code update changed everything. How to ensure your Juarez or Roma investment is earthquake-proof.

Explore juarez

Mexico City is a seismic zone. There is no getting around it. But the risk is not uniform. The 'Lakebed Zone' (Roma, Juarez, Condesa) amplifies waves, while the 'Hill Zone' (Lomas, Pedregal) is rock solid.

The Retrofit Imperative

Buying a 1900s mansion in Juarez? It likely needs a structural retrofit. We partner with top engineers to install viscous dampers or shear walls. This isn't just about safety; it's about asset value. A certified seismic-safe building commands a 15% premium.
