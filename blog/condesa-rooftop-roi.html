The ROI of Rooftops in Condesa

You can't build up, but you can build out. How activating the roof adds 30% to your property value.

Explore condesa

In Condesa, zoning laws strictly limit building heights to preserve the Art Deco scale. This makes the roof the most valuable underutilized asset in the neighborhood.

The Legal Path

While you often cannot add a 'structure' (walls and roof), you can add a 'light structure' (pergola) and decking. By turning a tar-paper roof into a lush garden with a wet bar, you effectively add a second living room.
